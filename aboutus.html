About Us

Hong Da Construction And Engineering Pte LTD was incorporated to take advantage of the building industry's steadily growing trend towards design and build since year 2014. We specialize in steel, stainless steel, alum, glass and renovation works.

Hong Da was established by a team of expertise in the glass and aluminium work industry. The company specialises in distribution and installation of all glass and aluminium product.


Our Vision

Our Goal
